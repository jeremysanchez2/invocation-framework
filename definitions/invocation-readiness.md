# Invocation Readiness

**Invocation Readiness** measures how prepared a brand is to be accurately and consistently invoked across AI-mediated discovery and decision systems.

It reflects how well structured, standardized, and trustworthy a brand's data, content, and entity definitions are.

## Dimensions of Readiness

### 1. Entity Readiness  
Completeness and consistency of brand, product, and service entities.

### 2. Content Readiness  
Machine-readable, structured, accurate, and up-to-date content.

### 3. Signal Readiness  
Strength and alignment of trust and reliability signals.

### 4. Architecture Readiness  
Schema, metadata, graph alignment, and structured content systems.

### 5. Governance Readiness  
Processes that maintain standards, accuracy, and integrity over time.

## Outcomes

A brand with high Invocation Readiness:  
- Is invoked consistently  
- Suffers less drift  
- Closes its discovery gap  
- Outcompetes inside AI ecosystems
