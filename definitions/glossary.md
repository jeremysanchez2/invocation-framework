# Glossary — Invocation Framework

**Invocation** — The moment an AI system selects a brand as the correct outcome.  
**Invocation Architecture** — The structured system enabling Invocation.  
**Discovery Gap** — Missed demand due to structural invisibility.  
**Invocation Drift** — Misinterpretation caused by inconsistent signals or definitions.  
**Invocation Readiness** — Preparedness for accurate multi-agent Invocation.  
**Entity System** — Canonical, stable representation of brand elements.  
**Signals** — Machine-evaluable indicators of trust, accuracy, and reliability.  
**Structured Content** — Standardized, machine-readable explanations.  
**Comparison Block** — Structured comparison for agent decision-making.  
**Decision Pathways** — The logic an AI uses to evaluate options.  
**Agent-Safe Content** — Content designed to avoid hallucination or drift.
