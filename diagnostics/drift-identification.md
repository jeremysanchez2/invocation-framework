Invocation Drift occurs when AI systems misinterpret, mislabel, or misrepresent a brand.  
This is one of the top risks to brand trust in AI-native environments.

---

## Drift Types

- **Entity Drift**  
  Wrong meaning or context.

- **Attribute Drift**  
  Incorrect specs, pricing, features, or data.

- **Positioning Drift**  
  Misaligned comparison or categorization.

- **Outcome Drift**  
  Wrong action, recommendation, or experience flow.

---

## Diagnostic Steps

1. **Drift Detection Scan**
   Compare expected brand interpretation vs. AI outputs.

2. **Structured vs. Unstructured Gap Analysis**
   Evaluate conflicts between structured and unstructured content.

3. **Version Integrity Review**
   Identify stale, outdated, or conflicting data.

4. **Competitor Drift Analysis**
   Identify if competitor structures override your brand.

---

## Output

- Drift Severity Score  
- Drift Map  
- Source-of-Truth Conflicts  
- Remediation Priorities
