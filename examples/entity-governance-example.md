# Entity Governance Example

This example demonstrates how a brand structures responsibility and ownership for maintaining Invocation Readiness.

## Brand: Patagonia

### Entity Owner
- VP of Digital Experience

### Data Stewardship Team
- Director of Content Operations  
- Sr. SEO Architect  
- Taxonomy Specialist  
- Knowledge Graph Engineer

### Governance Standards
- Brand entities verified quarterly  
- Product entities verified every 14 days  
- Schema updates deployed weekly  
- All structured content must pass:
  - Accuracy
  - Safety
  - Alignment
  - Readability
  - Machine Completeness

### Result
> Patagonia maintains one of the highest “Structured Content Stability Scores” in the outdoor retail sector, reducing Invocation Drift and strengthening brand reliability inside LLMs.
